Flying Snowflake,
The Long-tailed Tit

The long-tailed tit is a very small bird characterized by a round, white body, small black eyes, and a short beak. With an extremely cute appearance reminiscent of a flying snowflake or sticky rice cake, it is loved by many and called the 'fairy of the forest'.

They love living in flocks, and in the cold winter, they form a 'long-tailed tit train' by sticking closely together in a line on a branch to sleep and maintain body heat. They are active and curious, so just observing them is very healing. This photo book is filled with vivid daily lives of long-tailed tits through unreleased high-resolution photos.
